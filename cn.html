Computer Networks

Instructor: Cisco

Duration: 4 Weeks

This course provides a strong foundation in computer networking concepts. You will learn how devices communicate over networks, understand protocols, and explore real-world networking technologies used in modern systems.

Course Curriculum

  • Introduction to Computer Networks
  • OSI & TCP/IP Models
  • Network Devices & Topologies
  • IP Addressing & Subnetting
  • Routing & Switching Basics
  • Network Security Fundamentals
