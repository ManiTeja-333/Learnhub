Intro to AI Agents

Instructor: Udhbhav

Duration: 5 Weeks

This course introduces the concept of AI agents and how they interact with environments to perform intelligent tasks. You will learn how agents make decisions, solve problems, and are used in real-world applications like chatbots and automation systems.

Course Curriculum

  • Introduction to AI Agents
  • Types of Agents
  • Environment & Perception
  • Decision Making in AI
  • Agent-based Systems
  • Real-world Applications
